Ideathon

IIITD E-Cell presents you its event "Ideathon", an event which requires you to have only an open mind and a solution finding approach. It provides a platform to students to showcase their ideas and have a fair evaluation idea on the same in this covid time. It also helps students realize the position, pros, and cons of their idea which helps them in improving it.

Team Size : 1-4

PRIZES WORTH 15K WITH 10K CASH PRIZE ALONG WITH INCUBATION OPPURTUNITY AT IIITD INCUBATION CENTER.
The competition will take place in 2 rounds :

Round 1: In this round, all the teams have to submit an abstract of their idea which will be evaluated by our esteemed judges based on:
1. The innovation in your idea
2. The real-world problem it mitigates
3. The market opportunity for the idea.
The teams with the ideas which qualify the minimum eligibility criteria would be allowed to progress to the next round

Round 2: Top teams that clear the 1st round would be given the opportunity to pitch their ideas before a panel of judges. Each pitch would be 3 mins long and would be succeeded by a Q&A session with the judges
