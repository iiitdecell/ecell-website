Welcome to PitchCafé

Pitch Cafe nurtures the spirit of entrepreneurship amongst young innovators with budding ideas on startups. The goal of Pitch Cafe is to narrow down the gap between dreams and its implementation in real time. The event provides an excellent platform for early-stage startups and potential ideas to be recognized by the investors and consequently the industry. Further, it promotes innovative thinking and strengthens teamwork, leadership skills and interpersonal relationships amongst the young bright minds. Needless to say, Pitch Cafe is instrumental in enriching the entrepreneurship culture amongst the youth in the society.
